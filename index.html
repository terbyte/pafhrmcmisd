PAFHRMC History

PAFHRMC History

Philippine Air Force Personnel Management Center was established on 01 October 2007. It was organized and categorized as Air Force-Wide Support and Separate Unit. Consequently, on 22nd of November 2007, PAFPMC was formally activated with the mission: “To provide effective and efficient personnel management system in support to the Philippine Air Force mission”.

On 21 May 2020, the PAFPMC was renamed into Philippine Air Force Human Resource Management Center with the approval of Commanding General, Philippine Air Force, LTGEN ALLEN T PAREDES PAF with the mission: “to provide human resource management services in support to PAF mission”.

In June 2016, LTC ROBERT WENDELL N AUSTRIA PAF (GSC) assumed as the 15th Commanding Officer of PAFHRMC to continue the noble goal of effective and efficient Human Resource Management to address the needs of military and civilian personnel in the Philippine Air Force.

On 01 October 2007, marked the activation of PAFPMC and categorized as Air Force-Wide Support and Separate Unit pursuant to General Order Number 346. On 22 November 2007, PAFPMC was formally activated with the mission “to provide an effective and efficient personnel management system in support to the PAF mission”.

Moreover, on 21 May 2020, pursuant to General Order Number 403 the Philippine Air Force Personnel Management Center (PAFPMC) was renamed to Philippine Air Force Human Resource Management Center (PAFHRMC) with the mission “to provide human resource management services in support to the Philippine Air Force mission”.
